# Scope of Work

This document defines what is included in the Abcon Proposal and what is explicitly excluded.

## In Scope
The following elements are covered in this strategy proposal:
-   **Brand Context & Positioning:** Analysis of where each brand sits in the market.
-   **Directional Audits:** Topline review of current performance and creative assets.
-   **Audience Segmentation:** Definition of key target audiences for each brand.
-   **Go-to-Market Approach:** Strategic approach for taking each brand to market.
-   **Topline Recommendations:** Strategic suggestions to increase the volume and quality of leads.

## Out of Scope
The following elements are not included in this phase of work:
-   **Media Plans & Budgets:** Detailed media buying plans or specific channel budget allocations.
-   **KPI Targets:** Granular Key Performance Indicator targets (e.g., specific CPA or ROAS targets).
-   **Tool/Platform Implementation:** Setup or configuration of specific marketing tools.
-   **CRM/Process Redesign:** Restructuring of the sales process or CRM systems.
-   **Execution Planning:** Detailed timelines, project management, or resourcing plans for execution.
