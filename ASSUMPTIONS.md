# Assumptions and Constraints

To protect the strategic integrity of this proposal, the following assumptions and constraints have been applied.

## Data Access
-   **No Internal Data:** We do not have access to internal analytics, CRM databases, active ad accounts, or historical sales data.
-   **Public Insights:** All insights listed in this proposal are derived from public information, observable digital behaviour, independent market research, and industry benchmarks.

## Nature of Proposal
-   **Topline & Directional:** This proposal is intentionally high-level. It sets the strategic course rather than defining every tactical step.
-   **Exclusions:** Specific performance metrics, media budgets, and detailed performance forecasts are excluded at this stage.

## Validation
-   **Future Validation:** The assumptions made herein regarding audience behaviour and market potential may be validated and refined if access to internal data sources is granted in the future.
