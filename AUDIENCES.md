# Target Audiences

This document serves as the central reference for the target audiences and personas for each brand.

## Strive Property Specialists

### Audiences
-   CSuite decision makers
-   B2B property owners / investors
-   QSR & Food and Beverage operators
-   Retail and commercial tenants

### Persona Profile
-   **High-level demographics:** Professional, decision-making roles, high net worth or corporate stewardship.
-   **Core motivations:** Maximising asset value, reliability, operational efficiency, long-term partnership.
-   **Key concerns:** Vacancy rates, unreliable management, decreasing asset value.
-   **Typical decision drivers:** Trust, track record, service level agreements (SLAs), network reach.

## Abcon Developments

### Audiences
-   CSuite and institutional partners
-   B2B tenants
-   QSR & Food and Beverage groups
-   Industrial and motor sector operators

### Persona Profile
-   **High-level demographics:** Corporate leaders, institutional investors, business expansion managers.
-   **Core motivations:** Strategic growth, quality developments, timely delivery, innovation.
-   **Key concerns:** Project delays, cost overruns, poor build quality, location viability.
-   **Typical decision drivers:** Portfolio strength, development capability, financial stability, location strategy.
