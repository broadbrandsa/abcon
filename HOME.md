### Market Context: Why Digital Matters Now

Property decision-making has changed.

Across residential, retail, and commercial property, research now begins long before a conversation takes place. Buyers, tenants, and partners arrive informed, comparative, and time-conscious.

They expect:
- Clear information upfront  
- Proof of delivery and credibility  
- Fast, direct responses  
- Confidence before commitment  

Digital has become the first filter.

Those who communicate clearly, demonstrate authority, and respond quickly enter consideration early. Those who do not are often filtered out before a conversation ever begins.

For property groups with strong fundamentals, this shift represents an opportunity rather than a threat. Digital, when used intentionally, accelerates trust instead of replacing relationships.

### The Abcon Group Portfolio

The Abcon Group operates through three distinct but complementary brands, each serving a different role across the property lifecycle.

**Strive Property Specialists**  
The operational engine of the group. Responsible for property management, letting, and asset performance across retail, commercial, industrial, and residential portfolios.

**Abcon Developments**  
The value creation arm. Focused on the delivery of commercial, retail, industrial, motor, and mixed-use developments through partnerships and joint ventures.

**Craft Homes**  
The residential brand. Delivering homes across a wide range of price points, with a focus on quality, longevity, and trust for first-time buyers and growing families.

Each brand engages different audiences, faces different challenges, and requires a distinct digital approach. This proposal addresses them individually while recognising the strategic strength of the group as a whole.
